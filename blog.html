5 Critical Steps After Discovering Water Damage

When water damage strikes your home, taking immediate action within the first 24-48 hours is crucial for preventing long-term damage and mold growth. The five critical steps include ensuring safety by checking for electrical hazards and structural issues, stopping the water source by shutting off valves or patching leaks, documenting all damage through photos and inventory lists, contacting professionals including insurance and restoration services, and beginning initial mitigation efforts like removing valuables and excess water. Professional intervention is essential, as proper drying and restoration require specialized equipment and expertise to prevent secondary damage and ensure thorough recovery.
