Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to emergencies?

We guarantee a response time of 60 minutes or less for emergency calls within Orange County. Our fully-equipped teams are strategically located throughout the area to ensure rapid response 24/7/365.

What should I do while waiting for your team to arrive?

If safe to do so, turn off the water source and remove valuable items from affected areas. Don't use electrical appliances in flooded areas, and avoid walking on wet carpet to prevent damage spread.

How do you handle insurance claims?

We work directly with all major insurance providers and can handle the entire claims process on your behalf. Our detailed documentation and experienced team ensure smooth insurance claim processing.

How long does the restoration process take?

Timeline varies based on damage extent, typically 3-7 days for standard water damage. We provide detailed timelines after initial assessment and work efficiently to minimize disruption.

Do you guarantee your work?

Yes, we offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee and 5-year warranty on all our restoration work. If any issues arise post-restoration, we'll address them promptly at no additional cost.
